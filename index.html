My Projects



Battleships

Replica of the famous Battleships game made in Java for my ICS4U class.
It uses object orientated programming to make a 2 player and single
player version of the game in the same program.


Cat and Mouse

Variation of a Cat and Mouse game created with Java. A digital map 
is printed as the game runs. The user plays as the cat and has to catch all
the mice running away.


Grade 9 Introductory Course Videos

I had the initiative with one of my classmates to reach out to the 
guidance department in my school about helping Grade 8s in their transition 
to high school. So I brought a group of people together to collectively make 
short introductory videos for the many open courses that Grade 8s would have 
the option of choosing from. These videos were used by my school's guidance 
department when they went to present courses to our elementary feeder schools.

One of the departments represented by our videos was the technology department.
Where all of our Computer Science and Computer Engineering courses are situated in.
